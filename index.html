What We Do

We believe in providing prompt and customize services to our customers. To ensure this we give range of choices, based on the customer requirement our services are delivered.

Full truck Load

Parcel consignments

Parcel consignments service is for Distributors, Wholesalers, Retailers and for small scale Industries.



Express Trucking

Quick, on time & Reliable express trucking service for personalised Parcel & Consignments.

Truck Xpress is Indian Logistics Company established in the year 1989 in Faridabad, Haryana. It is under the parent company Sangam Transport company the name is acronym for Truck Express.



Sangam Transport company was started by Mohan Lal and the present Managing Director, we started our operations by providing services locally across the city and then gradually expanded its operations across the state, with the support of his brothers Mr Mohan Lal expanded its operations in Bangalore and Chennai.



Truck Xp today operates fromFaridabad, Haryana, providing its services in Bangalore, Chennai, Delhi, Jaipur, Jammu and Kashmir, Kolkata, Madhya Pradesh, Mysore and in Orissa.
